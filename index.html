«Green" trails for beginners — "Magic Carpet" and "White Bear", starting at the foot of large slopes in the immediate vicinity of the base station Shymkent.
"Blue" trails for more experienced skiers — "Uncle Bogdanovich", "Sunny Valley", "Zelenaya Polyana", working between the stations Levy Talgar and Talgar pass.
The "Red" trails for experienced skiers are the Olympic track running from the Konus station and the routes "Asiada", "Truba" and "Field of Miracles" starting from the Middle Station, as well as the highest mountain track of the Talgar resort, at an altitude of more than 3 kilometers.
"Black" tracks for professionals — "Crest", "Dry Log" and "Crest of the Twenty".
